Shaniwar Wada, Pune

About Shaniwar Wada

Shaniwar Wada is a historical fortification in the city of Pune, India. Built in 1732, it was the seat of the Peshwas of the Maratha Empire until 1818. The fort is a significant cultural landmark, offering a glimpse into the rich history of the Maratha rule.
