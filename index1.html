Marine Drive, Mumbai

About Marine Drive

Marine Drive is a 3.6-kilometer-long boulevard in South Mumbai. It is also known as the Queen's Necklace because of the street lights that make the road resemble a string of pearls in a necklace when viewed from an elevated point. Marine Drive is one of the most popular places in Mumbai, offering stunning views of the Arabian Sea, especially at sunset.
