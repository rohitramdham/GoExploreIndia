Red Fort, Delhi

About Red Fort

The Red Fort, located in the heart of Delhi, India, is a historic fortification that served as the main residence of the Mughal Emperors. Built by Emperor Shah Jahan in 1639, it is renowned for its massive enclosing walls of red sandstone and its intricate Mughal architecture, which includes a mix of Persian, Timurid, and Indian styles.
