Aga Khan Palace, Pune

About Aga Khan Palace

The Aga Khan Palace is a majestic building located in Pune, India. Built in 1892 by Sultan Muhammed Shah Aga Khan III, the palace is an important historical landmark. It played a significant role during India's freedom movement as Mahatma Gandhi, his wife Kasturba Gandhi, and his secretary Mahadev Desai were interned here following the Quit India Movement in 1942. The palace now houses a memorial dedicated to Gandhi and his philosophy.
