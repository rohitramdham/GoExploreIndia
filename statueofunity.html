Statue of Unity, Vadodara

About the Statue of Unity

The Statue of Unity is a colossal statue of Indian statesman and independence activist Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, located in the state of Gujarat, India. Standing at 182 meters (597 feet), it is the world's tallest statue. The statue is located on the Narmada River in the Kevadiya colony and is a major tourist attraction.
