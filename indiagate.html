India Gate, Delhi

About India Gate

India Gate is a war memorial located in the heart of New Delhi, India. It stands 42 meters tall and is dedicated to the soldiers of the British Indian Army who died during the First World War and the Third Anglo-Afghan War. Designed by Sir Edwin Lutyens, India Gate is one of the most prominent landmarks in Delhi, attracting both locals and tourists.
